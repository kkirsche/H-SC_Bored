Thursday, July 10 at 8 PM

LOCATION: Downtown
PRICE: $$
GET TICKETS: Click Here

View on Twitter

Back by popular demand, we’re bringing the best in NYC comedy to Half Lounge in Burlington. Our headliner is Jared Logan, who has performed a Half Hour special on Comedy Central and was a delegate for that network’s Indecision 2012 election coverage. He’s appeared on The Pete Holmes Show and can be seen on the upcoming season of The Meltdown, also on Comedy Central. The show also features NYC comic Kara Klenk, and local comics Phil Davidson and Carmen Lagala. Show starts at 8 and tickets are just \. Get your tickets in advance cause this will sell out. See you there!

Every Thursday at Half Lounge (136 1/2 Church Street Burlington)!

In 2013, Jared appeared in his own half hour stand-up special on Comedy Central You can see him every week as a cast member on VH1’s Best Week Ever. He’s been featured on TBS's The Pete Holmes Show and Comedy Central's The Meltdown. He was a delegate for Comedy Central's Indecision 2012. Check out his web series Don't You Think on YouTube. Jared lives in New York but is originally from West Virginia. He belongs to one of the few mountain clans who did not handle snakes or make moonshine.
